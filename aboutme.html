About me
Hi there! My name is Victoria Xu. I am a UI/UX designer based in Sydney, currently building a mobile and web application for Woolworths. I have been creating and designing experiences for 2 years now. I love bringing detail into everything I design and am a strong believer that life is all about colour!

When I am not designing, I am studying a Commerce and Economics degree at the University of New South Wales or travelling the world. It was through studying abroad that I discovered a passion for bettering customer experiences by using emerging tech and human centred design. Right now, I am fueled by anything (and anyone) that lets me learn new skills in these areas - so let me know if you're one of them!

I also enjoy watching a good movie and am an old soul when it comes to my love for jazz and picnics!
